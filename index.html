About Mariona

Born in Scotland, Mariona Muslek Katranis was encouraged by an artistic family to be creative. She studied Graphics at Glasgow College of Building & Printing before moving to Retail Visual Merchandising at Glasgow School of Commerce. After working in visual merchandising for several years she moved to Greece, where she worked in interior design, photographic styling and visual display. During this period she worked with many lucrative companies (including United Distillers Diageo and Adelcan S.A.) and was featured in Elle Decor for her interior projects. She now lives in Harrogate, England.

Her love of art in various forms and mediums — such as bronze, glass and wood — has remained constant in her career. She tends to express herself in large, life-size models and opulent plaques. Her designs have a sense of mysticism and tranquility. Her goal is to create beauty and a feeling of harmony through colour and vibrant, gleaming, hand-cut vitreous glass. She draws inspiration from various sources, including Margaret MacDonald Mackintosh, Gustav Klimt… and fairies.
