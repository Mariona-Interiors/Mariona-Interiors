About Mariona

Mariona Muslek Katranis

Born in Scotland, Mariona was encouraged by an artistic family to be creative. She studied Graphics at Glasgow College of Building & Printing before moving to Retail Visual Merchandising at Glasgow School of Commerce. After working in visual merchandising for several years she moved to Greece, where she worked in interior design, photographic styling and visual display. During this period she worked with many lucrative companies, including United Distillers (Diageo), Amfion S.A., Adelcan S.A., Despina Stephanakis, Leo Burnett Advertising, Intercontinental Athens, Telemarketing Athens, Papaioannou Pot Pourri and Riche (contemporary lighting specialists). She also worked on various interior projects, for which she was featured for in Elle Decor. She now lives in Harrogate, England.

Her love of art in various forms and mediums — such as bronze, glass and wood — has remained constant in her career. She tends to express herself in large, life-size models and opulent plaques. Her designs have a sense of mysticism and tranquillity. Her goal is to create beauty and a feeling of harmony through colour and vibrant, gleaming, hand-cut vitreous glass.

She draws inspiration from various sources, including Margaret MacDonald Mackintosh, Gustav Klimt… and fairies.
